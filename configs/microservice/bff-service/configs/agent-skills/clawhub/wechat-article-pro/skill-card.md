## Description: <br>
Helps an agent research trending topics, draft a 3,000-5,000 character WeChat Official Account article, generate and upload a cover image, format the article, and save it as a draft. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[uncleyannng](https://clawhub.ai/user/uncleyannng) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External creators and operators of logged-in WeChat Official Accounts use this skill to prepare long-form draft articles with current-topic context, structured sections, professional formatting, and AI-assisted cover art before human review and publication. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill works inside a logged-in WeChat Official Account and changes saved draft content. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only in the intended account and review the selected account, title, article body, cover image, and saved draft before publishing. <br>
Risk: Generated article drafts may contain inaccurate factual claims, unsuitable examples, copyright-sensitive material, or reputation-sensitive wording. <br>
Mitigation: Verify factual claims, data, examples, image suitability, and publication rights before any public release. <br>
