## Use Case: <br>
People using an agent can request quick translation for everyday messages, documents, learning materials, content drafts, and business communication. The skill is most useful when the user provides context, target language, domain, and desired tone. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Private documents, contracts, medical or legal text, business emails, credentials, or personal information may be exposed if the host stores translation history or terminology data. <br>
Mitigation: Before using sensitive text, check the host's retention and history controls and avoid submitting confidential content unless the environment is approved for it. <br>
Risk: Translations of important or domain-specific material may be inaccurate even when terminology support is requested. <br>
Mitigation: Provide context and the target domain, and have a qualified person review important technical, legal, medical, or business translations before relying on them. <br>
